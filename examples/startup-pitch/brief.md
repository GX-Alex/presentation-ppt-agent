# NovaTech AI — Company Brief

## Overview

**NovaTech AI** is an early-stage startup building AI-powered customer service automation for small and medium e-commerce businesses. Founded in January 2025 by three ex-ByteDance engineers, the company has completed angel round and is seeking a $5M Series A.

## Product

**SmartServe** — an AI agent platform that integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, and store API to:

- Automatically respond to ~70% of customer inquiries (order status, returns, shipping, product questions)
- Escalate complex issues to human agents with full context
- Analyze customer sentiment and flag at-risk orders
- Generate weekly CX (customer experience) reports with actionable insights

Pricing: $199/month base + $0.10 per escalated ticket. Average customer spends $320/month.
