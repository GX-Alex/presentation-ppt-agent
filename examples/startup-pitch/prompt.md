# Recommended Prompt

Copy and paste the following prompt into PresentationAgent's chat interface after uploading the brief or pasting its content.

---

## Prompt

```
I need a 10-slide pitch deck for a Series A fundraising presentation.

Source material: the attached brief about NovaTech AI.

Please create a clean, professional deck with the following structure:

1. **Title Slide** — Company name, tagline ("AI-Powered Customer Service for E-Commerce"), "Series A Pitch Deck · 2025"
2. **Problem** — E-commerce customer service is slow (avg 4h response), expensive, and doesn't scale for SMBs
3. **Solution** — SmartServe: AI agent that automates ~70% of inquiries, integrates in 15 minutes
4. **Product Highlights** — Key features: auto-response, smart escalation, sentiment analysis, CX reports
5. **Traction** — MRR growth chart (Jan → Aug), 91% retention, 47 merchants
6. **Market Opportunity** — $65B TAM, 28% CAGR in AI customer service
7. **Competitive Positioning** — 2x2 matrix: cheaper/faster vs Zendesk AI, Intercom Fin
8. **Business Model** — $199/mo base + usage pricing, $320 avg monthly spend
9. **Team** — Alice, Bob, Carol + key hires
10. **Ask & Use of Funds** — $5M Series A, breakdown: engineering, sales, SEA expansion

Design preferences:
- Modern, clean — avoid heavy icons or clip art
- Use a blue accent palette (#2563EB primary)
- Data points should be prominent (numbers, growth arrows)
- Keep text minimal — let visuals tell the story
```

## Alternative (Brief Version)

If you prefer brevity, this shorter prompt also works well:

```
Create a 10-slide Series A pitch deck from the attached NovaTech AI brief.
Slides: Title, Problem, Solution, Product, Traction, Market, Competition, Business Model, Team, Ask.
Professional tone, blue accent palette, data-forward design.
```
